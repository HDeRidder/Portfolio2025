SMART ENERGY MANAGEMENT

IoT-based Energy Optimization for Commercial Buildings

Project Overview

An intelligent energy management system designed for commercial buildings that utilizes IoT sensors and advanced analytics to optimize energy consumption. The system provides real-time monitoring, automated control, and data-driven insights to reduce energy costs and environmental impact.

Technical Implementation

  • Wireless IoT sensors for temperature, humidity, and occupancy
  • Smart meters for real-time power consumption monitoring
  • LoRaWAN network for long-range, low-power communication
  • Cloud-based analytics platform with machine learning capabilities
  • RESTful API for third-party integration
  • Automated HVAC and lighting control systems

Key Features

  • Real-time energy consumption monitoring
  • Predictive maintenance alerts
  • Automated peak load management
  • Occupancy-based climate control
  • Energy usage analytics dashboard
  • Mobile app for remote monitoring and control

Results

Implementation of the system resulted in a 30% reduction in energy consumption, 25% decrease in maintenance costs, and significant improvement in occupant comfort. The return on investment was achieved within 18 months of deployment.
