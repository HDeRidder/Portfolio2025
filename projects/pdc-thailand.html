PERMACULTURE DESIGN COURSE (PDC)

Learning Sustainable Agriculture in Thailand

Project Overview

This project involved taking a Permaculture Design Course (PDC) on an organic farm in Thailand. The course provided hands-on experience in sustainable agriculture, ecological design, and community living, with a focus on creating regenerative food systems and resilient landscapes.

Key Learnings

  • Principles of permaculture and ecological design
  • Soil health, composting, and water management
  • Agroforestry and integrated farming systems
  • Community building and collaborative problem-solving
  • Practical skills in organic gardening and natural building

Impact

The course deepened my understanding of sustainable agriculture and inspired me to integrate permaculture principles into future IoT and technology projects, especially those related to food production and environmental stewardship.
