I am a 25-year old optimist from Pajottenland, Belgium, fascinated by technology, innovation, and new ideas since childhood. Inspired by my father, I also have a deep love for nature, growing vegetables and restoring ecosystems, believing both are essential in a changing world.

Why IT and this orientation of choice?

My path to IT and IoT was driven by inherent creativity, inventiveness, and a constant curiosity fueled by books and hands-on experimentation.
